# What is Kiai?

Kiai is a **Discord leveling bot** that brings gamification to your server.  
It’s designed to **reward activity**, **encourage participation**, and **make communities more fun**.

## What Kiai Does
- Tracks activity like chat messages and voice time  
- Awards XP that builds toward levels  
- Unlocks rewards (roles, perks, or bragging rights)  
- Ranks members on leaderboards for friendly competition  

Servers love Kiai because it helps keep members engaged.  
Instead of conversations fading out, activity feels rewarding — and people come back for more.
