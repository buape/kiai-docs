# What’s New in Kiai

Kiai is always improving — new features, tweaks, and polish are released regularly.  

Here are a few highlights from recent updates:
- **Better Leaderboards** – cleaner visuals and smoother sorting make rankings easier to follow  
- **Custom Rewards** – admins now have more control over what perks members unlock as they level up  
- **Balanced XP Gain** – adjustments ensure activity like messaging and voice time feel more rewarding
